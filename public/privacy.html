Privacy Policy for LeetStats

Last Updated: February 2024

LeetStats ("we", "our", or "us") operates the LeetStats application. This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We use your data to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). This may include, but is not limited to:

  • Email address
  • First name and last name
  • Google profile photo

2. Use of Google User Data

Our application uses Google Authentication (OAuth) to verify your identity. The information we request from Google is strictly limited to your email address and basic profile information (name and picture). We use this information solely to:

  • Create a secure user account in our database (Supabase).
  • Associate your tracked LeetCode profiles to your specific account so you can view them across multiple devices.

We do not access your Google Contacts, Drive, Gmail, or any other private Google data.

3. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored in a PostgreSQL database managed by Supabase. We take reasonable measures to protect your Personal Data, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
